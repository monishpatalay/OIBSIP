Zayn Malik

About

Zayn Malik is a British singer and songwriter. He rose to fame as a member of the boy band One Direction, which was formed on the UK version of the X-Factor in 2010. One Direction quickly became one of the most popular and successful boy bands in the world, with a large fan base and several hit albums and singles. In March 2015, Malik announced that he would be leaving the band to pursue a solo career. After leaving One Direction, Malik signed a solo recording contract with RCA Records and released his debut album, "Mind of Mine", in March 2016. The album debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart and featured the hit single "Pillowtalk". He also had success with singles like "Dusk Till Dawn" and "I Don't Wanna Live Forever". Malik is also known for his fashion sense and has modeled for various fashion brands, and has been a judge of the singing competition show The X Factor. He is of Pakistani descent, and has been in a relationship with model Gigi Hadid. In 2018, Malik released his second album, "Icarus Falls", which features a mix of R&B, pop, and hip-hop styles. He also announced that he is working on his third album

Interesting facts about him

  • He was discovered on the UK version of The X Factor in 2010 and subsequently became a member of One Direction.
